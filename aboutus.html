RECRUITMENT PROCEDURE

We ensure proper selection in accordance with the employers' requirements. We follow the under mention

-Signing agreement between Planet Overseas and Foreign Employer

-Receiving visa slip, electronic wakala, tavia(identity card), and chamber of commerce certificate for Saudi Arabia

-Company should apply to the Labour Ministry of UAE for obtaining approval of required categories and quantites. Company will prepare the documents: 1. Power of Attorney 2. Demand Letter 3. Employment Contract 4. Documents Attested by Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Pakistani Authority in the host country

-To obtain permission from protector of emigrants, Govt. of Pakistan, and advertise in national newspapers

-Screening-Interview-Trade Testing for technical/skilled workers from Govt. approved and well reputed technical institutions

-Medical Examination of selected workers

-Preparation of documents to embassy for stamping visa and endorsement of visa

-After receiving of endorsement of visa, workers are asked to make NICOP(Govt. of Pakistan)

-Make reservation of air tickets and confirm to our valued client by fax or email

-Departure of emigrants
